Once Upon a Time in a Friendly Little Wood...

In a soft, sunlit corner of the world, where the trees lean in to listen and the paths remember every pair of little feet, word has begun to rustle through the leaves. The friends of the Wood have heard the happiest news: a tiny new adventurer named Gunner will soon be joining their story.

The trees are humming, the clouds are drifting a little slower, and even the honey pots seem to shine more brightly than usual. It’s not every day a new chapter begins, and certainly not every day a baby is welcomed into the heart of such a cozy place.

“I’m quite sure a day this special needs a table full of snacks, a sky full of friends, and just one more taste of something sweet.”
– our honey-loving bear from the edge of the Wood

So the friends made a Very Important Decision: they would gather everyone—two-legged, four-legged, fluffy, feathery, and everything in between—for a celebration filled with stories, laughter, and the kind of warmth that comes from knowing you’re already loved, even before you arrive.

This invitation is your page in that story. Turn it gently, and see what the Wood has planned for Baby Gunner and for you.

Of Bounces, Giggles, and Gentle Chaos

Some friends prefer quiet corners and soft chairs. Others prefer seeing just how high joy can bounce before gravity brings it back down. For Baby Gunner’s celebration, there will be a bit of both.

Light games, gentle laughter, and the kind of silliness that makes even the grown-ups forget about their grown-up faces for a while. You’re welcome to join in every game or simply enjoy watching the bouncing from a comfortable, snack-filled distance.

“The wonderful thing about celebrations is that there’s room for every kind of happy – the quiet kind, the laughing kind, and the sort that bounces all over the place.”
– our endlessly bouncy striped friend
Little Games & Gentle Fun
We’ll have sweet, baby-themed games planned for those who love to play, with plenty of space for chatting, sipping, and soaking it all in for those who don’t.
Snacks & Treats
A table sprinkled with sweet things and savory bites – a little something for every honey-loving heart, with options for those who prefer something light.

Gifts, Books & Cuddly Things

Your presence is the part that matters most. Truly. But for those who have kindly asked, Baby Gunner’s parents have tucked a few ideas into a small registry and a simple request: if you’d like, bring a favorite children’s book to sign instead of a card, so bedtime can be filled with the voices of the people who loved him first.

Registry
A small registry of helpful, practical things has been prepared for anyone who wishes to browse. Details will be shared with your invitation or via text.
A Library for Little Ears
In place of a traditional card, you’re invited (if you’d like) to bring a favorite children’s book and sign the inside cover. One day, a little pair of ears will listen, and a little heart will know your name.
“Arms full, heart full, pouch full. That’s the proper way to welcome someone small, I think.”
– our caring kangaroo and her curious little joey

Friends Around Every Corner

The Wood is full of characters, each with their own way of loving, worrying, bouncing, listening, and caring. In the same way, this celebration is filled with all different kinds of hearts – quiet ones, loud ones, steady ones, and wonderfully silly ones. Together, they make the kind of welcome a baby remembers without ever having to remember it at all.

Honey Bear
Thinks best with a snack in hand and believes every baby deserves a day that feels like a warm blanket.
Little Pig Friend
Small in size, large in courage. Nervous at first, but fiercely proud to show up for the ones he loves.
Bouncing Tiger
Turns any gathering into a celebration and thinks “quiet time” just means softer bouncing.
Gloomy Donkey
A little droopy, a little dry, and endlessly loyal. The one who always shows up, even when he’s sure no one will notice.
“I suppose they’ll have the celebration whether I’m there or not. But I’ll be there. New friends don’t come along every day.”
– our quietly loyal grey friend

And so, this little chapter of the story draws to a close, but the bigger one is only beginning. Baby Gunner is already surrounded by more love than the Wood could possibly count, and your presence in this celebration makes that love even louder.

We cannot wait to welcome you into this cozy, honey-sweet moment in our family’s story.
