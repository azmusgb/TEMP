Sweet Party Favors

As a special thank you for celebrating with us, we've prepared honey jar favors for each guest. At your place setting, you'll find a small glass jar filled with warm amber local honey, topped with a matte metal lid and a hand-tied natural twine bow.

Each jar features a tiny kraft tag with the handwritten message "Sweet as can bee" - a little reminder of our sweet celebration and the even sweeter addition to our family.

Don't forget to grab your jar and a bee-themed cookie on your way out. Thank you for making our day so special!

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Gunner's baby shower

What should I wear to the baby shower?

The dress code is casual and comfortable. We're embracing the Winnie the Pooh theme with honey and pastel colors, so feel free to wear something in those color families if you'd like to join in the fun!

Is there parking available at the venue?

Yes, there is ample parking available at Sunnybrook House. There's a large parking lot directly adjacent to the venue with plenty of spaces for all our guests.

Can I bring my children to the shower?

While we love all the little ones in our lives, this will be an adults-only event so we can all relax and enjoy the celebration. We appreciate your understanding!

What kind of food will be served?

We'll be serving a delightful assortment of honey-themed treats, light finger foods, beverages, and of course, a beautiful Winnie the Pooh-themed cake! Please let us know about any dietary restrictions when you RSVP.

When should I RSVP by?

Please RSVP by November 28, 2025 so we can finalize our arrangements. You can RSVP right here on this website!
