Once Upon a Time in the Hundred Acre Wood...

Welcome to the Hundred Acre Wood

In a corner of the world where stories grow on trees and adventures wait around every bend, a very special celebration is being prepared. The friends of the Hundred Acre Wood are gathering together, for they've heard the most wonderful news a new friend is coming to join their adventures!

"Oh, bother! I do believe this calls for a Very Special Something. Perhaps with a little honey?"

And so it was decided that all the creatures of the wood would come together for a Grand Celebration to welcome this new little friend. They would share stories, play games, and of course, enjoy plenty of honey and treats. For when a new friend arrives in the Hundred Acre Wood, it is always a cause for the happiest of gatherings.

Answers to Curious Questions

Questions Answered

Even in the most wonderful stories, characters sometimes have questions. Here are answers to some things you might be wondering about.

What should I wear?
Think "comfortable storybook character" - soft yellows, gentle blues, creamy whites, or whatever makes you feel most like yourself. The dress code is "cozy and celebratory." No formal attire required!
Are children welcome?
Absolutely! We're designing this as a family-friendly celebration. There will be activities and spaces specifically for little ones. Please indicate children in your RSVP so we can plan accordingly.
What about gifts?
Your presence is the present! If you'd like to bring something, we've shared registry information, but handwritten notes or favorite children's books are equally treasured.
Can I come for just part of the time?
Of course! We understand busy schedules. Come when you can, stay as long as you're able. Every moment shared is precious to us.
What if I have dietary restrictions?
Please mention any dietary needs in your RSVP. We'll have clearly labeled options for common restrictions and can accommodate most needs with advance notice.
Is there a theme for gifts?
Soft blues, honey golds, and storybook themes would be lovely, but honestly, the most meaningful gifts are those given with love. Practical items are just as welcome as whimsical ones.
What's the parking situation?
Plenty of parking available! Follow the honey pot signs to our special guest parking area. Golf cart assistance is available if needed.
Who should I contact with questions?
Text Calab at (302) 922-0740 or Lea at (609) 828-7347. We're happy to answer any questions about the celebration!
