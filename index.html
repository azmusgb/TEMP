Event overview

How the day will feel

A soft, layered room: powder-blue base, honey-gold accents, plenty of white and greenery, and character-inspired touches that stay elegant, not loud.

Imagine walking into a bright, airy room with eight round tables, each styled around a different beloved character. Instead of cartoon prints, every table features a 3D printed figurine, soft florals, and a powder-blue runner so the space still reads as a cohesive baby boy shower.

  • Soft linens in white and ivory, layered with powder-blue gauze runners.
  • Honey-gold cutlery, candlelight, and small touches of greenery on each table.
  • Subtle blush or accent colors by table (Piglet gets a hint of pink, Eeyore gets dusty blue, etc.).
  • Photo-friendly layout: you can stand at one corner and see all tables in one sweeping view.

Guest details

Logistics & quick answers

All the tiny practical things guests usually text about — answered in one calm spot.

What should I wear?
Think soft & comfortable — powder blues, creams, tans, or sage. Dressy casual is perfect. No one needs to dress up more than they want to.
Can I bring kids?
Yes. Children are welcome, but the overall vibe is still adult-friendly and relaxed. We’ll have a few snacks and quiet activities set aside for little ones.
Is parking easy?
Street and driveway parking are both available near the house. If you need help getting inside with gifts or little ones, just text when you arrive.
What if I can’t stay the whole time?
Totally fine. Drop in for hugs, a plate of food, and a moment with Mom & Baby. The schedule is flexible by design.
