Overview at a glance

This is the quick spec for florists, rental teams, and 3D printing: how many tables, how many characters, and what colors anchor each zone.

8 round guest tables
6–8 seats each · ~60 guests total
Honey at every place
60–70 small jars with twine & tags
Florals per table
1 main vase + 1–2 bud vases each
8 3D-printed figurines
4–6" tall · one character per table
Decorator’s blueprint

Per-table styling character by character

All tables: round · white floor-length cloth · powder-blue gauze runner in a loose S-curve · rattan or wood chargers · white plates · clear stemless glasses · brushed gold flatware · honey jar on each plate with twine and “Sweet as can bee” tag.

Bear
Bear Table
Warm · Honey-focused
Figurine
Chubby golden-brown bear, muted red shirt, ~4–6" tall on blue pedestal.
Florals
White daisies, small yellow blooms, soft greenery.
Napkins
Honey-yellow napkins, simple fold on or under plates.
Accents
Optional extra honey jar at center, nodding to “Sweet as can bee.”

This is your most literal honey table—good candidate for near dessert or main cake viewing angle.

Piglet
Piglet Table
Soft · Reassuring
Figurine
Shy piglet, light pink body, dusty-rose striped jumper, blue pedestal with blush rim.
Florals
Pale pink roses, white blooms, baby’s breath, light greenery.
Napkins
Dusty-blush napkins, soft but not neon; still reads boy-friendly with blue runner.
Accents
Small blush gauze “puddle” under pedestal atop blue runner for layered texture.

This table carries the softest pink in the room; seat VIP guests who like gentle palettes.

Donkey
Donkey Table
Cool · Calm
Figurine
Droopy-eared donkey in dusty blue-grey, seated on darker blue pedestal.
Florals
Dusty-blue blooms, white daisies, eucalyptus.
Napkins
Slate-blue or grey-blue napkins.
Accents
Optional “patched” detail on pedestal to echo his stitched charm.

Use this as a visual “quiet zone” among the brighter, warmer tables.

Tiger Cub
Tiger Cub Table
Playful · Lively
Figurine
Orange tiger cub with soft brown stripes, curled tail, blue pedestal with orange ring.
Florals
Bright yellow and orange flowers with white highlights.
Napkins
Soft marigold or honey-orange napkins.
Accents
Subtle spiral or “bounce” texture on pedestal base.

Place where kids will sit or where you want high-energy photos; reads joyful but not chaotic.
