Chapter One ⋆⋆⋆

Once Upon a Time in the Hundred Acre Wood...

A little boy named Gunner was on his way, and all of Pooh's friends gathered to plan the sweetest storybook shower.

In the heart of a sunny clearing, where the breeze smells faintly of honey and the creek sings a gentle song, a wonderful rumor began to spread...

"Did you hear?" whispered Piglet, clutching his scarf a little tighter. "There's a new little someone joining the story!"

Pooh tilted his head, thinking very hard (which is something he does slowly). "A Very Small Boy with a Very Big place in our hearts," he decided.

And so, the Hundred Acre Wood began to bustle with simple, happy preparations—not for just any ordinary day, but for Gunner's Woodland Adventure.

Chapter Three ⋆⋆⋆

A Few Familiar Faces in the Wood

These friends won’t be at the party in person, but their spirit is woven into every detail.

Pooh

Official Honey Taster & Chief Comfort Officer

Pooh reminds us that “sometimes the smallest things take up the most room in your heart.”

Piglet

Keeper of Gently Brave Thoughts

A little nervous about big changes, but always the first to say “Yes, let’s.” Just like new parents.

Tigger

Energy Consultant & Jumping Coordinator

For when sleep schedules get creative and everyone needs a reminder that bouncing back is possible.

Eeyore

Realistic Expectations Specialist

The one who gently reminds us that some days will be hard, and that it’s okay not to have it all together.

Owl

Advice Giver (Whether You Asked or Not)

Has many ideas and a few very long stories, but occasionally, a helpful tip lands just right.

Roo

Official Reminder That Little Ones Grow Fast

Roo is here to keep us playful, curious, and aware that baby days are fleeting and very precious.
