Our Story

Welcome to our enchanted corner of the Hundred Acre Wood, where every day is an adventure and every friend is family.

Once upon a time, in a cozy corner of the Hundred Acre Wood, a new adventure began. Little Gunner arrived, bringing with him the sweetest honey and the warmest cuddles the wood had ever known. For one whole year, he's been exploring, learning, and filling our days with laughter and wonder.

From his first giggle (which sounded remarkably like a happy little bear) to his determined crawls through the meadow (always following the scent of honey, of course), every moment has been a page in this beautiful storybook.

A Year of Firsts

Just like Christopher Robin's adventures with Pooh, Gunner's first year has been filled with magical firsts: first smiles that could rival the sunniest day in the Wood, first steps that were more exciting than any expedition to find Heffalumps, and first words that were sweeter than the purest honey from Pooh's favorite pot.

Winnie the Pooh

"Sometimes the smallest things take up the most room in your heart."

Winnie-the-Pooh, or Pooh for short, is an anthropomorphic, honey-loving teddy bear. He is a loyal friend to Christopher Robin and the other animals in the Hundred Acre Wood.

While he may not be the smartest or most graceful bear, Pooh has a heart of gold and often stumbles upon wisdom in his simple, honey-focused way of life.

  • Loves honey more than anything
  • Best friends with Piglet
  • Lives in a house under the name "Mr. Sanders"
  • Known for his thoughtful "Oh, bother" moments
  • Has a little red shirt that he always wears
